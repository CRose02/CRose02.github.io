Backseat Burnouts (2024). Backseat Burnouts is a retro, voice proximity-enabled online karting game that allows up to 8 people to race on fast-paced, head-to-head racetracks. Relive the nostalgia of early 2000s couch-based racers, invite your friends, and laugh at their misfortune as you use items to get ahead, distort everyone's voices, and boost your way through the finish line!

Developed by Watch This Space Studios as our university capstone project.
